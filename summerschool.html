Industrial Agents Summer School

The TC-IA organizes the last couple of years a summer school focusing on Industrial Agents and their application.

  • 2024 — 10th International Summer School on Industrial Agents, 24 – 28 June 2023, Bilbao, Spain.
  • 2023 — 9th International Summer School on Industrial Agents, 26 – 30 June 2023, Valenciennes, France.
  • 2022 — 8th International Summer School on Industrial Agents: Autonomous Control in logistics, production, and energy applications, 11 – 15 July 2022.
  • 2021 — 7th International Summer School on Industrial Agents: Embedded Intelligence within the Edge and Fog Computing, 21 – 25 June 2021, [ONLINE].
  • 2020 — 6th International Summer School on Industrial Agents: Engineering of Cyber-Physical Production Systems, 29 June - 3 July 2020, University of Warwick, Coventry, UK.
  • 2019 — 5th International Summer School on Industrial Agents, 1-5 July 2019, Bragança, Portugal.
  • 2018 — 4th International Summer School on Industrial Agents & Cyber-Physical Systems, 11-18 May 2018, Saint Petersburg, Russia.
  • 2017 — 3rd International Summer School on Intelligent Agents in Automation, 20-28 July 2017, Emden, Germany.
  • 2016 — 2nd International Summer School on Intelligent Agents in Automation: The 4th Industrial Revolution and the Industrial Internet of Things, 9-17 June 2016, Linköping, Sweden.
  • 2015 — 1st International Summer School on Intelligent Agents in Automation, 27-30 July 2015, Lisbon, Portugal.

If you want that your institution organizes such an Industrial Agents summer school, please get in touch with the TC-IA chair. Bid submissions are to be done each May for the next year. Check the detailed info on organizing and carrying out a summer school.
