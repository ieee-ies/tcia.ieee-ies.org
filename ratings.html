List of High-quality Conferences and Scientific Journals in Industrial Agents Domain

The publication in high-quality conferences and scientific journals assumes a crucial manner to disseminate the state-of-the-art, the new trends and innovative approaches and results, and the industrial and prototype applications in industrial agents.
The IEEE IES Technical Committee on Industrial Agents has been performing the identification of the most important high-quality international conferences and scientific journals related to the agents domain, that can constitutes guidance for the people working on agents to publish and present its work.
For this purpose, it is purposed two distinct lists, one for scientific journals and another to Proceedings of Conferences, each one organized in classes according to their importance, impact factor and relevance for the topic.

Scientific Journals
The list related to the scientific journals comprises three classes:
  • A: including the 50% high scored ISI indexed journals with relevance to the industrial agents domain. A special sub class A+ is considered to include the 20% top ranked journals.
  • B: including the lower 50% high scored ISI indexed journals with relevance to the industrial agents domain.
The current list of identified with relevance to industrial agents domain is the following:
