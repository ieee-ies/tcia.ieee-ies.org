Books

The members of the TC-IA on Industrial Agents have published several books relevant to the areas of the TC.

Industrial Agents: Emerging Applications of Software Agents in Industry [2015]
The book can be used to introduce the main concepts and examples in the domain of Industrial Agents.

Industrial Cloud-Based Cyber-Physical Systems [2014]
The book presents cutting-edge emerging technologies and approaches in the areas of service-oriented architectures, intelligent devices and cloud-based cyber-physical systems. It provides a clear view on their applicability to the management and automation of manufacturing and process industries.

Managing Complexity [2014]
The book describes large-scale complex adaptive systems, and their application to practical business problems to yield excellent returns on investment.

Design for the Unexpected: From Holonic Manufacturing Systems Towards a Humane Mechatronics Society [2015]
The book presents new, even revolutionary, ideas to managing production and production systems which may fundamentally shift the paradigm of manufacturing systems design.

Distributed Control Applications: Guidelines, Design Patterns, and Application Examples with the IEC 61499 [2015]
The book provides an overview of using the IEC 61499 reference model for distributed automation in various domains.

Internet of Things: Technologies and Applications for a New Age of Intelligence [2018]
The book outlines the overall vision for the IoT and Cyber Physical Systems (CPS), also touching on emerging technologies, such as device communication and their interaction, physical instrumentation of devices to the cloud infrastructures, IoT data collection, analytics and business impact.
